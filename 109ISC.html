About This Event

The 109th Indian Science Congress is all about looking at science and technology from a global perspective to create a sustainable future.

Description

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is privileged to host the 109th Indian Science Congress for the second time. This year's focal theme, "The Global Perspective on Science & Technology for a Sustainable Future", highlights the critical role of international collaboration in leveraging science and technology to address today's most pressing challenges.
